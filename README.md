> **"Your corneas' absolute best friend. Zero accounts. Infinite forced squares."**

Have you ever wanted to host a ridiculous meme online without handing over your blood type, your mother's maiden name, and your first pet's social security number to a data-hungry tech giant? Welcome to **PHOTO FOX**.

1. **Retinal-Relaxation Matrix:** The background shifts colors dynamically and subtly. It is clinically unverified but highly suspected to stop your eyes from melting out of your skull during 3 AM browsing sessions.
2. **Visual Communism (The Grid):** Whether your photo is tall like a majestic giraffe or wide like a panoramic camera mistake—it *will* become a 1:1 perfect square. Resistance is futile. Uniformity is peace.
3. **Ghost Telemetry:** No log in. No sign up. If you upload a photo and lose the link, godspeed, because we have absolutely no way to help you find it. You are a ghost to us.
4. **"Beauty in Titlement" Reel:** A dynamic rolling news ticker that spits out hyper-dramatic, serious-sounding system notifications to make your monitor look like a 90s hacker movie.

### 🚀 How to Summon the Fox
1. Clone this chaotic masterpiece.
2. Install the lightweight spine: `npm install express multer cors`
3. Wake up the beast: `node server.js`
4. Open `index.html` and let the visual engineering consume you.
